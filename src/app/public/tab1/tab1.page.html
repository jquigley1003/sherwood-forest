Welcome to Sherwood Forest Civic Association

Welcome! Sherwood Forest neighborhood is an urban forest within Fulton County in the City of Atlanta. Established in 1949, Sherwood Forest was once one of the premium post WWII neighborhoods. Our neighborhood consists of 200 homes with the majority being owner occupied.

Sherwood Forest Civic Association is an active community organization maintaining the essential business of the neighborhood and providing fun events to encourage and promote social engagement.

Fun Facts

 Sherwood Forest was originally known as Collier Woods. Collier Woods spanned some 2000 acres with Sherwood Forest being the last area to be developed. The original Collier house still stands within our neighborhood and has recently been nominated as a building of historical importance by the Atlanta Preservation Center.
