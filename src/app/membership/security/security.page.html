Security Information

Sherwood Forest is one of the safest neighborhoods in metro Atlanta. SFCA board takes the safety and security of neighbors, property, and visitors seriously. We have four active license plate readers at each entrance. We also have paid off-duty police officers on random shifts to maximize coverage patrolling our neighborhood.

Emergency: Dial 911.

Introducing Lt. Gary L. Harper

Lieutenant Harper (Gary) is a 22-year veteran of the City of Atlanta, currently serving in the Contingency Operations Division. He oversees protests, large permitted events, filming in the city, and primary planner for critical incident management in the City of Atlanta.

Prior to his Atlanta Police career, he served in the United States Army; a decorated Desert Shield/Storm Veteran, Paratrooper, and Military Police officer.

"As an active member in my own community, I am aware of what is expected and am committed to providing the same service I expect from my own neighborhood patrol."

"Some general notes that you may have heard:"

I am a firm believer in the "See Something, Say Something" model. Feel free to call, text, or email any information you feel is relevant to your neighborhood safety. (keep in mind that your first call should be to 9-1-1) Lock your vehicles Remove valuables from your vehicle Speak to your neighbors Turn on porch/driveway lights Clear excessive growth from your windows Your safety is a partnership with your community and the Atlanta Police Department, BE VIGILANT!!
