Security Information

Sherwood Forest is one of the safest neighborhoods in metro Atlanta. SFCA board takes the safety and security of neighbors, property, and visitors seriously. We have four active license plate readers at each entrance. We also have paid off-duty police officers on random shifts to maximize coverage patrolling our neighborhood.

Emergency: Dial 911.

Introducing Lt. Jim Hodge

My name is Lt. Jim Hodge and I am your neighborhood’s main security patrol officer. I would like to introduce myself to you all, provide my direct contact information and give you an update regarding my hours and recent police activity. It was a great pleasure to meet many of you at the summer picnic and look forward to meeting many more of you at other events as well as during my patrols. Please feel free to wave me down to say hi when you see me in the neighborhood.

My direct contact, specifically for the patrol in your neighborhood is:
Email: sherwoodforestofficer@gmail.com
Phone: 404-558-3808

Please feel free to contact me directly, at any time with matters of concern. I will do my very best to promptly reply with a solid answer or at least an artful dodge. I’ll provide information and will reach out to the Video integration unit regarding “hits” from the license plate cameras located at each of the entrances to Sherwood Forest.

With permission of the SFCA board, I plan to send out the following on a regular basis:

1. My schedule for the upcoming week. 2. Any crime updates that are relevant to Sherwood Forest, edited to protect privacy. 3. Answer any questions you have for myself or APD in general 4. Any relevant announcements and updates.

My hope is that by providing information directly to you, via email, your newsletter and Facebook page I will be able to keep you better informed as to what is happening in and around your neighborhood while keeping the privacy of victims and witnesses protected.
