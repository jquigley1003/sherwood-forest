Security Information

Sherwood Forest is one of the safest neighborhoods in metro Atlanta. SFCA board takes the safety and security of neighbors, property, and visitors seriously. We have four active license plate readers at each entrance. We also have paid off-duty police officers on random shifts to maximize coverage patrolling our neighborhood.

Introducing Officer Brian Beebe

Many of you already know Ofc. Beebe as he has been handling security at Ansley Golf Club for over 20 years. Ofc. Beebe is a POST certified officer with the APD. Ofc. Beebe also has other officers that he may bring in from time to time to cover patrol hours, although he will be our primary officer. We are excited to have Ofc. Beebe on board.

For those of you that do not know him, he is approachable, accessible, and close by. We are confident he will be a highly visible presence in the neighborhood. I encourage you all to say hello when you see Ofc. Beebe in the neighborhood. He drives a Dodge Charger with Atlanta Police magnets on the door.

"Some general notes that you may have heard:"

If you see something, say something. Call, text, or email any information you feel is relevant to your neighborhood safety. Keep in mind that your first call should be to 9-1-1 Lock your vehicles Remove valuables from your vehicle Speak to your neighbors Turn on porch/driveway lights Clear excessive growth from your windows Your safety is a partnership with your community and the Atlanta Police Department, BE VIGILANT!!
